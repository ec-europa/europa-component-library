## Your gateway to the European Union

<Paragraph size="lead">
  As the Gateway to the European Union, europa.eu provides up-to-date,
  consistent and relevant information on the over-arching aims and objectives of
  the European Union, its membership, operational structure, services and key
  actions.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph size="lead">
  The information is structured and displayed to facilitate easy interaction,
  and build onward engagement through clear connections to further relevant
  content wherever it may lie throughout the EU digital ecosystem.
</Paragraph>
